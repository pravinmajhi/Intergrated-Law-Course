Courses Offered

BA LLB

The BA LLB course integrates the study of law with the study of humanities, providing a broad educational foundation and specialized legal knowledge. This five-year program combines academic learning with practical training, preparing students for various legal careers.

  • Duration: 5 Years
  • Core Subjects: Constitutional Law, Criminal Law, Corporate Law, Environmental Law
  • Career Opportunities: Lawyer, Legal Advisor, Judge, Corporate Legal Consultant

BBA LLB

The BBA LLB course merges business administration with legal education, equipping students with managerial skills alongside legal knowledge. This five-year program prepares students for a career in legal consultancy, corporate law, and business management.

  • Duration: 5 Years
  • Core Subjects: Business Law, Corporate Management, Finance, Marketing
  • Career Opportunities: Corporate Lawyer, Legal Manager, Business Consultant, Compliance Officer
